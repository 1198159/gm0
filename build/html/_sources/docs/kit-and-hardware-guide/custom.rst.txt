======
Custom
======
Many teams elect to ignore kits entirely and instead make their own mechanisms 
completely from scratch. 
While this **allows for much more design freedom**, 
keep in mind it **has many caveats and drawbacks**, 
especially for a new team. 
Additionally, a lot of teams choose to create a robot that is a mix of kit parts
and custom parts.
