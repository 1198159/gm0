Electronics¶

Introduction¶

The FTC control system is based on using Android phones as a “Robot Controller” and a “Driver Station.” The two major control systems of FTC are Modern Robotics and REV; however, nearly all teams are shifting over to REV because it is cheaper, simpler, and more reliable.
